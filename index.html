1 Introduction

Introduction

Welcome to my personal academic site！ My name is Qiwei Zhang, a master student in China University of Geosciences (Beijing). My primary research orientation is quantitative petrology. I prefer to use multidisciplinary approach including geochemistry, igneous petrology, thermodynamic modeling, and field work to investigate the fundamental chemical and physical processes that control magma evolution at depth as well as shallow plumbing systems underlying active volcanoes.

I will use this site to post my recent research about ternary phase diagram, the fractionation process of high silica granite and some computer programs I made. Here is my Curriculum Vitae. If any questions come up, please don't hesitate to contact me through my email:qiwei.zh@foxmail.com.
